Q. What Is UPSC Civil Service Examination?

The goal of this test is to churn out the finest brains to take over one of the most renowned services of India, the Civil Service.

Q. What should we do to become an IAS IPS officer?

Ans. There are two of the most prestigious All-India services, the Indian Administrative Service (IAS) and the Indian Police Service (IPS). Every year, the Civil Service Examination is held by the Union Public Service Commission in order to select officers at the all-India level for the IAS and IPS.

In order to become an IAS or IPS officer, you must complete the following steps:

  • Read the UPSC CSE Notification attentively.
  • Know the test syllabus, qualifying requirements.
  • Go through the UPSC exam timing schedule. Know the test dates, date of application and other key dates.
  • Apply for UPSC CSE prelims test on UPSC official website.
  • Study for the CSE test.
  • Clear the prelims examination.
  • Apply for mains examination by filling out the Detailed Application Form (DAF) issued to you online by UPSC.
  • Study well and pass the MAINS exams.
  • Try to score as many marks in mains as you can.
  • Do well at the interview stage.
  • Wait until the CSE results are proclaimed.
  • Pass the last round of interviews.
  • Secure a respectable rank.
  • Become an IAS/IPS Officer.
